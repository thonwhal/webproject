  • Meal-Prep Curried Chicken & Chili-Lime Chicken

Combine brown rice and quinoa; divide among 4 single-serving containers with lids. Top with chicken, cilantro and scallions, dividing evenly. Seal containers and refrigerate for up to 4 days.
